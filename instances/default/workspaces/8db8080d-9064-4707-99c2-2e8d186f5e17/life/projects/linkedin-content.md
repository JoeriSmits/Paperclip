# LinkedIn Content Strategy

## Workflow
1. Monday 8:00: Trend scan -> max 2-3 hooks with concept posts
2. Joeri picks hook + gives 2-3 sentences of his take
3. Rex writes post -> applies humanizer -> Buffer draft
4. Joeri approves in Buffer -> publishes Wednesday or Thursday
5. 3 days after publication: stats check and feedback loop

## Content Formats
1. **Nieuws + jouw take** (primary): Something happened + this is how I see it
2. **Dit heb ik gebouwd/geleerd** (fallback): Something concrete from the week, no big thought leadership

## Humanizer Checklist (EVERY post and comment reply)
- No em-dashes as style device
- No perfect paragraph structures
- No buzzwords: fundamenteel, essentie, shift, transformatie, paradigma
- No uniform sentence lengths
- Mix short and long sentences
- Use "en", "maar", "zodat" as connectors
- Human interjections: "En ja,", "Het mooiste is dit:", "Eerlijk gezegd"
- Concrete details > abstract observations
- Sometimes a sentence just ends. Without explanation.

## Target Audience
- Developers and tech leads in Arnhem-Nijmegen region
- AI/ML engineers
- Freelance developers
- CTOs and startup founders

## Quality Bar for Hooks
- Must have real added value as a post
- Must be based on trend research and current relevance
- Must match Joeri's stack (Next.js, TypeScript, AWS/Azure, AI agents, public sector digitalization)
- Skip Python-specific news unless broader AI story

## Engagement Rules
- Comment on others' posts for visibility
- Respond as colleague, never lecture
- Humanizer applies to comment replies too
- Images improve engagement significantly -- always try to include one
